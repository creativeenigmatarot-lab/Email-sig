A Vision in Progress

My art for this deck is a unique mash-up of the nostalgic, rebellious spirit of Garbage Pail Kids mixed with the whimsical, colorful world of My Little Pony from my childhood. The discovery of tarot has helped me find a deeper sense of self-worth, and that personal transformation is the magic I want to share with others through this deck.

The Major Arcana: Cards 0-5

Each card is a story. Here's a look at the first six cards of the Major Arcana and a bit about the meaning behind them.

The Fool

​The Fool: A leap of faith into the unknown, guided by pure heart. The Fool represents new beginnings, unbridled potential, and the courage to start a journey without knowing the destination. This is your first step into a world of magic, self-discovery, and a little bit of madness.

The Magician

The Magician: Everything you need is within you. The Magician represents a moment of pure focus and will, channeling the raw energy of the universe to turn potential into a powerful reality.

The High Priestess

The High Priestess​: Beyond what you can see lies a world of secrets. The High Priestess stands as a gatekeeper to the subconscious, urging you to trust your gut feelings and seek the truth that lies just beneath the surface. This card is a reminder that the answers you seek are already within you.

The Empress

The Empress: The Empress is the embodiment of wild creativity and lush abundance. She is a reminder that your power to create is limitless, and that nurturing your passions is the first step to bringing your dreams to life. This card invites you to embrace the fertile, vibrant energy of the universe and watch your ideas blossom.

The Emperor

The Emperor: ​The Emperor represents ultimate authority and control. This card is about establishing a firm foundation for your ambitions, setting your own rules, and mastering your own reality. It's the disciplined power you need to build your own empire and take charge of your destiny.

The Hierophant

The Hierophant: ​The Hierophant represents a search for deeper meaning and the wisdom of established systems. This card is about finding a sacred path or a teacher to guide you, and understanding that spiritual truth can be found in unconventional places and from unlikely mentors.
